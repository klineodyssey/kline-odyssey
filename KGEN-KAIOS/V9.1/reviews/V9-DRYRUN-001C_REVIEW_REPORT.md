## Review Summary

The DRAFT responds to unemployment increase and proposes citizen support plus profession balancing. The source concern is valid, but the task is too broad for a single worker execution path and mixes multiple sensitive domains: citizen support, profession training, employment balancing and identity-adjacent constraints.

## Review Findings

- Dependencies exist.
- No protected path change is requested.
- The task is still simulation-only, so Human review is not required at the current R2 classification.
- The acceptance criteria are too broad for one WorkOrder.
- The task should be split into smaller DRAFTs before execution:
  - Citizen training simulation review.
  - Profession-to-business balance report.
  - Employment signal dashboard analysis.

## Codex Decision

Codex rejects this DRAFT in its current form. The idea may return as smaller WorkOrders with narrower scope, clearer acceptance criteria and stronger boundary language.
