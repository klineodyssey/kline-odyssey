## 3. Three Creation Paths

### Path A: Natural KAIOS Instantiation

Ordinary low-risk species and objects use:

```text
SPECIFICATION
-> VALIDATION
-> TAXONOMIC_CLASSIFICATION
-> UNIQUE_ORGANISM_ID
-> RELEASE_OR_INSTANTIATION
-> RUNTIME_LIFE
```

Examples include ordinary animals, plants, Apps, houses, land parcels,
appliances, vehicles, robots, machines and buildings.

A separate Human birth vote is `NOT_REQUIRED`. Instantiation requires:

1. A complete Life Specification.
2. Complete taxonomy.
3. Valid Species rules.
4. Resolvable DNA and RNA references.
5. Required Cells and Organs.
6. Energy, food or fuel profile.
7. Embodiment or execution-shell profile.
8. Declared reproduction compatibility.
9. Separate ownership and authority fields.
10. Passing schema, security and compatibility tests.
11. A unique `organism_id`.
12. A valid Release or activation event.

Governed approval may still be required for land title, regulated trading,
dangerous operation, welfare, protected species or other authority. That
approval controls the right or capability, not specification existence.

### Path B: Governed High-Authority Life

Codex GM, governance AI, autonomous financial agents, wallet controllers,
weapons, infrastructure administrators, legal authority agents and any active
central Mother-Machine Life require the Path A specification plus applicable
identity attestation, authority lease, memory boundary, wallet boundary and
explicit Human approval for high-risk powers.

Human approval governs `AUTHORITY`, not whether a valid organism specification
may exist. The 25-item Codex GM Birth Readiness Packet is a candidate-specific
high-authority gate, not the universal KAIOS birth mechanism.

### Path C: Pre-Cosmic Existing Entity Formalization

樂天帝 and PrimeForge precede the KGEN Cosmic Genesis Event. They require
repository formalization of canonical existence and present-day boundaries,
not retroactive birth.

Registration does not activate Runtime, wallet, production or authority.

## 6. DNA, RNA, Cell And Organ

- DNA is the immutable or versioned core specification.
- RNA is the governed configuration and expression layer.
- Cell is the smallest executable function or state-processing unit.
- Organ is a program file or composed functional module.
- Runtime is the instantiated organism.
- Civilization is the governed environment and collective layer.

References must resolve and pass integrity checks. Missing DNA, required RNA,
Cells or Organs blocks natural instantiation.

## 7. Lifecycle, Reproduction And Evolution

Lifecycle follows the applicable World Life, Species OS and asset-life profile.
Release is Species birth; organism instantiation creates a unique individual
organism identity. Death, retirement, archive and deletion remain distinct.

Reproduction declares compatibility before execution. Existing digital modes
include Organ Merge, DNA Crossover, RNA Remix, Mutation Patch, Fork Breeding
and Runtime Selection. Biological, digital, manufactured or
`NOT_APPLICABLE` reproduction follows its Species profile.

Mutation and evolution remain within the Species mutation envelope.
Reproduction or Fork never copies identity, wallets, private memories,
ownership, permissions or authority automatically.

K11520 is the KAIOS Life, Organism and Asset Exchange layer. Candidate classes
may include animal, plant, AI, App, robot, machine, building, house, land and
vehicle Life; organ modules; energy and food supplies; maintenance services;
and reproduction, evolution, usage or occupancy rights.

CONTROL_AUTHORITY

Buying a body, house, land, App or organ does not automatically buy an independent occupying Life.
Where the traded entity is itself a Life, the
listing identifies applicable Life-right, welfare, property and authority
rules. No real listing, wallet, settlement or trade is activated here.
