Bang, including:

The KGEN Big Bang is the cosmic genesis event involving universe formation,
coordinate-space formation, and initialization of laws, dimensions, energy,
time, and civilization substrate.

entities. It is not the birth event of 樂天帝 or PrimeForge.

biological species, descendants, reincarnated Lives, and embodied robot Lives.

rights, and authority boundaries.
