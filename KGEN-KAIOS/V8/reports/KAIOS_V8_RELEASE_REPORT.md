## Release Purpose

V8.0 turns the one-picture-one-temple Canon into an operational KAIOS layer. Codex can read a player's current asset state, identify missing modules, and generate a governed WorkOrder roadmap for Cursor or another Worker. The system supports entry from a picture, land, residence, temple, App, real business, or complete civilization node.

## Compliance Position

V8.0 is not a live commercial marketplace, bank, securities platform, payment processor, or brand integration. Real-world business links require legal authorization, business agreement, payment compliance, consumer protection, tax compliance, audit readiness, and security review. Regulated assets remain Future Regulated Module.

## Next Phase

The recommended next phase is V8.1 Machine-Readable Task Graph, converting V8 WorkOrders and task generator rules into JSON for KAIOS Dashboard ingestion and automated status visualization.
