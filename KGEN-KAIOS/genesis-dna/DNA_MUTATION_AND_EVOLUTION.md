# DNA Mutation and Evolution

## 1. Evolution Axes

- `EvolutionXP`: unbounded verified experience.
- `TrainingLevel`: LV1-LV1000 progress within a training curriculum.
- `GenomeGeneration`: G0, G1, G2 and onward, recording lineage depth only.
- `DNAQualityGrade`: D, C, B, A, S, SS or Genesis, scoped to one reviewed revision.
- `active_atom_count`: 0-108 verified active atoms.

None of these values is a substitute for another.

## 2. Atom Unlock Flow

```text
LV1..LV1000 training
-> threshold reached
-> evidence submitted
-> anti-cheat and provenance check
-> Sandbox Evolution
-> Compatibility Test
-> Integrity Test
-> Balance Test
-> Quality Review
-> Activation Decision
-> next atom ACTIVE or request REJECTED
```

A threshold grants eligibility to request an atom, not the atom itself.

## 3. Mutation Contract

A Mutation records `mutation_id`, parent blueprint, purpose, changed genes/modules, expected expressions, affected atoms, risk class, energy/resource delta, environment assumptions, rollback, creator, reviewer and status. Production parents are never overwritten.

## 4. Digital Reproduction

Digital reproduction means Capability Recombination, schema-compatible merge, skill inheritance, memory-approved transfer, plugin composition or lineage fork.

```text
Parent Selection
-> Compatibility Check
-> Trait Selection
-> Sandbox Birth
-> Test
-> Review
-> Species Registration
-> Human Approval when risk requires
-> Activation
```

It never means direct source overwrite, Canon pollution, secret inheritance, unauthorized private-memory copying or cross-tenant DNA contamination.

## 5. Evidence Sources

Evidence may include mission success, reliability, customer rating, quality score, repair rate, attendance, contribution, production output, farm yield, marketplace reputation and reviewed backtest results. Domain relevance is mandatory. A finance backtest cannot prove agricultural yield or social capability.

## 6. Anti-Gaming

Duplicate commits, self-dealing, circular evidence, repeated missions, synthetic customer identities, copied reports, hidden test exclusions and cherry-picked windows do not earn duplicate XP or atoms. Evidence must identify source, time window, environment, owner, signer/reviewer and confidence.

## 7. Regression and Retirement

A capability may become `MAINTENANCE_DUE`, `SUSPENDED` or `RETIRED` when evidence expires, compatibility breaks, resource budgets fail or behavior changes. Historical XP and lineage remain immutable; active count is recalculated from active atoms only.
