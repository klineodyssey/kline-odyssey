# Player DNA Creator Standard

## 1. Allowed Creations

A player may propose a DNA Blueprint, Species Variant, Organ Variant, Behavior Package, Farm Yield Package, AI Strategy Package or Building Organism Package. Creation does not grant Canon, production or marketplace authority.

## 2. Governed Flow

```text
CREATE
-> DECLARE PURPOSE
-> SELECT PARENT GENOME
-> MUTATION DESIGN
-> SANDBOX BIRTH
-> INTEGRITY CHECK
-> BALANCE TEST
-> ENVIRONMENT TEST
-> ETHICS / SAFETY REVIEW
-> CODEX QUALITY GATE
-> HUMAN / GOVERNANCE APPROVAL when high risk
-> REGISTRATION
-> LICENSE
-> ACTIVATION
```

## 3. Tunable Parameters

Architecture may expose `growth_rate`, `yield`, `fruit_count`, `maturity_time`, `reproduction_rate`, `feed_efficiency`, `disease_resistance`, `climate_tolerance`, `water_requirement`, `energy_requirement`, `lifespan`, `movement_speed`, `strength`, `memory_capacity` and `task_efficiency`.

Every value is constrained by Species limits, energy/resource conservation, environmental impact, population balance, disease/mutation risk, game-economy balance, land capacity and review. No free max-all configuration exists.

## 4. Creator Request

Required draft fields include `creator_request_id`, creator/owner roles, purpose, parent genome, target species, desired traits, parameter deltas, expected benefit, cost budget, license intent, privacy class, safety statement, evidence plan, test plan and rollback plan.

## 5. Pricing

Transparent prices may cover sandbox compute, test runs, storage, facilities, review service, listing preparation, maintenance and license fees. Pricing must show customer payment, creator revenue, reviewer fee, platform fee, royalty, refund and net settlement.

No price purchases false performance, a fake GA, an untested grade, Yudi/Rulai status or a gate bypass.

## 6. Yield and Market Balance

Yield changes are limited by input resources, land carrying capacity, time, weather/environment, labor, storage, transport, spoilage, demand and market absorption. Controls include diminishing returns, dynamic maintenance cost, output cap, cooldown, ecological externality score, regional supply stress, anti-duplication evidence and rollback.

## 7. Player Equality

Every eligible player follows the same evidence and safety gates. Provider, wealth, family, title or early access cannot reserve GA107/108. Paid resources can improve testing throughput, not the truth of evidence.

## 8. Zero-State Boundary

No creator account, blueprint, payment, species, atom activation or listing is instantiated by this proposal.
