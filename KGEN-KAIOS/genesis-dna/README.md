## Fixed Boundaries

- Atom Count is `0..108`; Evolution XP is unbounded; Genome Generation is `G0..`; DNA Quality Grade is separate; Training Level is `LV1..LV1000`.
- GA means `Genesis Capability Atom`, never a generic level, rank, morality score, inherited caste or real chemical atom.
- Inherent life capabilities remain available through Species Genome and Life OS without being counted as evolutionary GA.
- Real genetic engineering, laboratory procedures, pathogens, real CRISPR parameters and harmful biological instructions are forbidden.
- Proprietary formulas, private engine packages, credentials, KYC and precise GPS never enter this public package.
- K11520 compatibility means governed rights or license listing eligibility, not automatic securities status or ownership transfer.
