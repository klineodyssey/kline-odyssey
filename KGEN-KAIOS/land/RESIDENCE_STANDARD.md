# Residence Standard

## Fields
- residence_id
- home_parcel
- owner_id
- tenant_id (optional)
- protection_class: CORE_HOMELAND | RESIDENTIAL_PROTECTED | STANDARD

## Protection
- CORE_HOMELAND and RESIDENTIAL_PROTECTED must not permanently transfer due to a single civilization conflict.
- On governance transfer: Owner unchanged; Tenant unchanged; no arbitrary eviction/confiscation.
- Postwar: civilian_protection, tax_transition_period, rent_protection, reconstruction_fund, appeal_window, audit_log.
