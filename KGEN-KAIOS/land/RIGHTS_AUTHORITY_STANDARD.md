# Rights Authority Standard

## Separable Authorities
| Field | Role |
|-------|------|
| owner_id | Private ownership |
| governor_id | Governance |
| tax_authority_id | Tax collection |
| defense_authority_id | Defense / border |
| airspace_authority_id | Airspace |
| orbital_authority_id | Orbital |

## Rules
- Four+ rights may be held by different actors.
- Governance change does not change Owner or Tenant by default.
- New Governor may collect governance tax under tax_cap rules only.
