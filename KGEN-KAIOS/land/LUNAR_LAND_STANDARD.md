# Lunar Land Standard

## Principle
Moon / K16888 uses an **independent celestial body and land registry**.

## Unlock on arrival (not ownership of whole Moon)
Outpost, Research, Mining, Energy, Trade, Housing, Defense, Logistics.

## Rules
- Earth Land Registry must not silently absorb lunar parcels.
- Same rights-separation and residence-protection architecture applies.
- Documentation / prototype only.
