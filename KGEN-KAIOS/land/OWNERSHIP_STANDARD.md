# Ownership Standard

## Principle
Player object and Land object are separate.

## Player may reference
home_parcel, residence_id, owned_parcels, leased_parcels, farmland_rights, commercial_rights, airspace_rights, orbital_rights, underground_rights.

## Rules
- Title / Realm Governor does not auto-own all private parcels.
- CORE_HOMELAND / RESIDENTIAL_PROTECTED cannot permanently transfer from a single conflict.
- Ownership transfer requires explicit registry process + Human Governance Policy when applicable.

## Commercial rights
`commercial_rights` may be held independently of fee-simple ownership; architecture only.
