# Territory Zone Standard

## Zone Types
- `CIVIL_ZONE`
- `RESIDENTIAL_SAFE_ZONE`
- `NON_WAR_ZONE`
- `BORDER_ZONE`
- `CONTESTED_ZONE`
- `WAR_ELIGIBLE_ZONE`
- `FRONTIER_ZONE`
- `WILDERNESS`
- `RESOURCE_ZONE`
- `MYTHIC_REALM`

## Behavioral Rules
- CIVIL_ZONE: no civilization combat; living/work/trade/agri/housing OK.
- RESIDENTIAL_SAFE_ZONE: homes and basic living facilities protected.
- NON_WAR_ZONE: diplomacy / commerce / governance competition only.
- BORDER_ZONE: border defense, customs, transit, economic competition.
- CONTESTED_ZONE: governance/resource rights reallocable via campaign sim.
- WAR_ELIGIBLE_ZONE: gamified campaigns; protected residences must not be destroyed.
- FRONTIER_ZONE / WILDERNESS: explore, settle, outposts, new civilizations.
- RESOURCE_ZONE: extraction under quota/royalty.
- MYTHIC_REALM: narrative overlay; still under residence protection.

## Zone Change
Proposal → Resident Impact Review → Governance Review → Human Approval → Registry Update.

Landlord cannot unilaterally convert inhabited civil zones to war zones.
