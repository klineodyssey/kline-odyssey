# KAIOS V8.2 Governance Signal Standard

## Purpose

Governance Signal Standard converts economy activity into readable civilization health signals.

## Signals

| Signal | Meaning |
|---|---|
| GDP | Prototype measure of total production and market activity. |
| Population | Active Citizen and NPC count. |
| Employment | Citizens with active profession and employer. |
| Temple Activity | Temple service usage, missions, citizen links and points. |
| Market Activity | Listings, transactions, simulated volume and price discovery. |
| Civilization Health | Weighted score combining economy, population, resources, governance and risk. |
| AI Activity | AI services, reports, validations and App assistance. |

## Signal Rules

1. Signals are simulation metrics.
2. Signals must name data sources.
3. Signals must include confidence and update time.
4. Signals cannot claim market price prediction or investment performance.
5. Signals should trigger governance review when risk, unemployment, reserve stress or dispute rises.
