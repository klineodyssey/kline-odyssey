# Market Runtime

**Runtime ID:** KAIOS-V8.2-RUNTIME-MARKET  
**Status:** Prototype / Simulation

## Purpose

The Market Runtime groups transactions into a market view and produces simulated price discovery. It does not place real orders, create financial recommendations or connect to live liquidity.

## Supported Operations

- Buy simulation.
- Sell simulation.
- Auction concept.
- Order Book concept.
- Price Discovery.
- Liquidity concept.

## Price Discovery Inputs

- Resource supply.
- Citizen demand.
- Business inventory.
- Temple activity.
- Market activity.
- Governance risk signal.

## Output

- Reference price.
- Market activity score.
- Liquidity mode.
- Listing quality warning.
- Dispute state.

## Boundary

Auction, order book and liquidity are concept or simulation layers. Regulated markets require future authorization, compliance review and production controls.
