# Governance Signal Runtime

**Runtime ID:** KAIOS-V8.2-RUNTIME-GOVERNANCE-SIGNAL  
**Status:** Prototype / Simulation

## Purpose

The Governance Signal Runtime converts economy activity into civilization governance indicators. These signals help Codex, Cursor and future KAIOS workers determine whether a civilization node is healthy, blocked, overextended or ready to upgrade.

## Signals

- GDP.
- Population.
- Employment.
- Temple Activity.
- Market Activity.
- Civilization Health.
- AI Activity.

## Derivation

GDP is a simulated aggregate of business revenue and market activity. Population is read from citizen records. Employment is the ratio of citizens with active profession assignments. Temple Activity is derived from temple-linked actions. Market Activity is derived from transaction frequency, listing count and resource movement. Civilization Health is a weighted score that also includes risk warnings.

## Review Use

Governance signals may recommend:

- New business WorkOrders.
- Supply chain repair.
- Market risk review.
- Treasury review.
- Citizen profession balancing.
- Civilization upgrade readiness.
