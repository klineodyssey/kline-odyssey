# KAIOS V8.2 Consumption Standard

## Definition

Consumption is the use of resources, goods, services, Apps, AI compute, temple services or civilization rights by Citizens, Businesses, Temples or Civilization systems.

## Consumption Types

| Type | Consumer | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Citizen Consumption | Citizen | Food, clothing, medicine, hotel, education. |
| Business Consumption | Business | Energy, materials, logistics, AI compute. |
| Temple Consumption | Temple | Temple points, service materials, App maintenance. |
| AI Consumption | AI | Data, compute, memory scope. |
| Civilization Consumption | Civilization | Public infrastructure, governance, security. |

## Rules

1. Consumption reduces inventory or capacity.
2. Consumption may improve health, energy, skill, reputation, productivity or civilization health.
3. Consumption must be traceable to a transaction, service event or internal allocation.
4. Regulated consumption such as medical, financial, insurance or real-world service delivery requires legal boundary metadata before Production.
