# KAIOS V8.2 Supply Chain Standard

## Flow

```text
Producer -> Logistics -> Warehouse -> Retail -> Consumer -> Recycling
```

## Nodes

| Node | Function |
|---|---|
| Producer | Creates food, materials, data, knowledge, energy, Apps or services. |
| Logistics | Moves inventory or service access between locations. |
| Warehouse | Stores inventory, materials, equipment and App licenses. |
| Retail | Sells or rents goods and services to citizens or businesses. |
| Consumer | Uses or consumes goods, services, Apps or resources. |
| Recycling | Converts used items into lower-grade materials or data signals. |

## Rules

1. Every supply-chain transfer must reference source, target, resource, quantity and status.
2. Logistics may be simulated before Production.
3. Real-world delivery requires business agreement, consumer protection, tax and delivery compliance.
4. Recycling may create materials, reputation or civilization health signals.
