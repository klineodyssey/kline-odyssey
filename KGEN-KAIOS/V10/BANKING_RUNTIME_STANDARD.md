# Banking Runtime Standard

**Document ID:** KAIOS-V10-BANKING-RUNTIME-STANDARD  
**Version:** V10.0  
**Status:** Simulation Only

Banking Runtime models treasury and reserve logic for KGEN civilization simulation. It is not a real bank, not a licensed financial service and not a deposit product.

## Simulation Modules

- bank account profile
- treasury
- deposit simulation
- withdrawal simulation
- reserve ratio
- loan concept
- interest concept
- collateral concept
- insurance concept
- audit trail
- KYC / AML boundary
- risk control

## Regulated Boundary

Any real financial service requires legal authorization, licensed partners, jurisdictional compliance, consumer protection, audit and human approval.
