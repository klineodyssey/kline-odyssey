# NFT Standard

**Document ID:** KAIOS-V10-NFT-STANDARD  
**Version:** V10.0  
**Status:** Concept

NFT is a possible future representation layer for land, membership, app, equipment or proof rights. V10 does not mint NFTs or define any live tokenized asset sale.

## NFT Candidate Domains

- Land proof
- Temple proof
- App organism proof
- Membership badge
- Equipment
- Event certificate
- Real-world twin proof, subject to legal authorization

## Boundary

NFT must not be used to imply securities, ownership of real-world assets, brand partnership, real estate rights or regulated investment without legal review.
