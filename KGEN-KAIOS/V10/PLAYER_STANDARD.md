# Player Standard

**Document ID:** KAIOS-V10-PLAYER-STANDARD  
**Version:** V10.0  
**Status:** Draft for Review

Player is the human-facing identity in the KGEN universe. Player is distinct from Citizen, Wallet and Membership. One player may control multiple citizens, apps, wallets or businesses depending on permissions.

## Player Fields

- player_id
- member_id
- display_name
- avatar
- reputation
- linked_wallet_profiles
- owned_temples
- owned_land
- owned_businesses
- active_citizens
- app_inventory
- risk_flags
- audit_refs

## Lifecycle

Guest exploration -> registered account -> verified member -> owner/operator/developer/governor roles. Every role transition must be auditable.
