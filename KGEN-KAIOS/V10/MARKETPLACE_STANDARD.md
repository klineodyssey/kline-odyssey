# Marketplace Standard

**Document ID:** KAIOS-V10-MARKETPLACE-STANDARD  
**Version:** V10.0  
**Status:** Prototype / Concept Boundary

Marketplace defines how KGEN assets may be listed, discovered and reviewed. V10 does not execute real trades.

## Asset Categories

Land, Temple, Residence, Store, App, AI, DNA, GA Strategy, Equipment, Materials, Civilization Technology, Membership and Virtual Business Twin are modeled as possible marketplace assets.

## Listing Requirements

- listing_id
- asset_id
- owner
- asset_type
- metadata
- valuation
- pricing_model
- disclosure
- transfer_rules
- rental_rules
- legal_restrictions
- dispute process

## Regulated Boundary

Equity, tokenized rights, real-world business rights, fiat payment and real asset transactions are Future Regulated Modules only.
