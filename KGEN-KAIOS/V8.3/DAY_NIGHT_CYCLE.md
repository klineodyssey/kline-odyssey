# Day Night Cycle

**Document ID:** KAIOS-V8.3-DAY-NIGHT-CYCLE  
**Status:** Draft for Review / Prototype

## 1. Purpose

The Day Night Cycle gives citizens, businesses and temples a predictable daily rhythm. It affects activity, energy, service availability, consumption and market demand.

## 2. Day Phase

Day phase increases:

- Citizen work.
- Business production.
- Market activity.
- Logistics movement.
- Temple service visits.
- Learning and professional training.

## 3. Night Phase

Night phase increases:

- Rest.
- Energy recovery.
- Security monitoring.
- Inventory audit.
- Temple quiet activity.
- Maintenance and archive tasks.

## 4. Entity Effects

| Entity | Day Effect | Night Effect |
|---|---|---|
| Citizen | Work, movement, trade, learning | Rest, energy recovery, reduced market demand |
| Business | Production, retail, service | Audit, restock, repair |
| Temple | Service, population attraction | Ritual memory, low-intensity faith accumulation |
| Market | Higher activity | Lower activity, settlement review |
| Resource | Consumption rises | Regeneration may improve in some zones |

## 5. Boundary

This cycle is simulation-only. It does not control real-world stores, labor schedules, payments or legal obligations.
