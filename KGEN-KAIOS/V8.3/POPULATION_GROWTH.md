# Population Growth

**Document ID:** KAIOS-V8.3-POPULATION-GROWTH  
**Status:** Draft for Review / Prototype

## 1. Purpose

Population Growth models the long-term movement of civilization life. It includes attraction, migration, growth, retirement and archive states.

## 2. Population Inputs

- Temple Activity.
- Residence capacity.
- Business employment.
- Resource availability.
- Market stability.
- Civilization Health.
- Safety and disaster signals.
- Governance response.

## 3. Growth Channels

- Migration.
- Citizen training.
- Residence expansion.
- Profession creation.
- Conceptual reproduction layer.

## 4. Conceptual Reproduction Boundary

Reproduce is a life-cycle concept inherited from the KGEN App and Citizen model. It is a simulation abstraction. It does not describe real human biological data, medical systems or personal identity generation.

## 5. Population Outputs

- Population delta.
- Employment demand.
- Residence demand.
- Resource pressure.
- Market demand.
- Governance signal.
