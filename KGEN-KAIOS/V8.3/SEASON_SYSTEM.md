# Season System

**Document ID:** KAIOS-V8.3-SEASON-SYSTEM  
**Status:** Draft for Review / Prototype

## 1. Purpose

The Season System gives the civilization longer waves of change. Seasons affect resource regeneration, citizen mood, business demand, temple festivals and governance pressure.

## 2. Official Seasons

| Season | Simulation Meaning | Economy Impact |
|---|---|---|
| Spring | Growth and discovery | Higher food growth, migration and learning |
| Summer | Production and market heat | Higher commerce and energy demand |
| Autumn | Harvest and governance review | Higher inventory, tax and festival activity |
| Winter | Conservation and resilience | Lower production, higher reserve demand |

## 3. Season Signals

Each season may modify:

- Resource regeneration.
- Business demand.
- Citizen energy.
- Population growth.
- Temple activity.
- Market activity.
- Disaster probability.
- Festival probability.

## 4. Governance Use

Seasonal changes help Codex and future KAIOS workers detect whether a civilization is resilient across time rather than only strong in one snapshot.
