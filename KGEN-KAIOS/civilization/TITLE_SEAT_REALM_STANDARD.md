# Title / Seat / Realm Standard

## Model Fields
realm_id, k_anchor, seat_id, title_id, title_name, governor_id, authority_scope, tax_scope, defense_scope, appointment_method, challenge_rule, term_rule, succession_rule, revocation_rule

## Example K11520
- Realm: 花果山
- Seat: 水簾洞
- Title: 美猴王
- Authority Type: CAVE_LORD / REALM_GOVERNOR

Represents governance/tax/mission/border/build/reputation — **not** automatic ownership of all private land, home confiscation, permanent immunity, or real-world legal power.

Also: K12345 五指山; K18888 凌霄寶殿; K23333 靈山.

Title ≠ Land Ownership.
