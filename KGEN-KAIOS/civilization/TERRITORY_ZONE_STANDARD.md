# Territory Zone Standard (Civilization)

Zones (cite Land Runtime; civilization applies economic/conflict rules):

- `CIVIL_ZONE`
- `RESIDENTIAL_SAFE_ZONE`
- `NON_WAR_ZONE`
- `BORDER_ZONE`
- `CONTESTED_ZONE`
- `WAR_ELIGIBLE_ZONE`
- `FRONTIER_ZONE`
- `WILDERNESS`
- `RESOURCE_ZONE`
- `MYTHIC_REALM`

CIVIL_ZONE: no combat; living/work/trade/agri/housing.
RESIDENTIAL_SAFE_ZONE: homes protected.
NON_WAR_ZONE: diplomacy/commerce/governance competition.
BORDER_ZONE: border defense, customs, transit, economic competition.
CONTESTED_ZONE / WAR_ELIGIBLE_ZONE: campaign sim with residence protection.
FRONTIER_ZONE / WILDERNESS: explore/settle.
