# AI Temple Advisor

**Document ID:** KAIOS-V9.0-AI-TEMPLE-ADVISOR  
**Status:** Draft for Review / Prototype

## Purpose

The Temple Advisor reads Temple Activity, faith value, population attraction and civilization influence to recommend temple support actions.

## Inputs

- Temple Activity.
- Faith value.
- Population attraction.
- Civilization influence.
- Festival readiness.
- Citizen energy.
- Economy state.

## Recommendations

The advisor may recommend:

- Temple upgrade draft.
- Citizen support.
- Festival readiness review.
- Service quality improvement.
- AI support review.

## Boundary

Temple recommendations are simulation and governance suggestions. They do not claim religious authority, real-world partnership or guaranteed economic value.
