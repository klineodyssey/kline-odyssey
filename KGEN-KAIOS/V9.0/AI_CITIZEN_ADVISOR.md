# AI Citizen Advisor

**Document ID:** KAIOS-V9.0-AI-CITIZEN-ADVISOR  
**Status:** Draft for Review / Prototype

## Purpose

The Citizen Advisor reads citizen energy, employment, profession, reputation, migration and consumption signals.

## Inputs

- Citizen State.
- Profession State.
- Residence.
- Employer.
- Business behavior.
- Energy state.
- Skill and reputation.
- Population growth.

## Recommendations

The advisor may recommend:

- Citizen support.
- Profession training.
- Work/rest balancing.
- Migration review.
- Employment matching.
- Draft WorkOrder for citizen schema or dashboard improvement.

## Boundary

Citizen recommendations are simulation-only. They do not represent real employment, medical, financial or identity decisions.
