# AI Economy Advisor

**Document ID:** KAIOS-V9.0-AI-ECONOMY-ADVISOR  
**Status:** Draft for Review / Prototype

## Purpose

The Economy Advisor reads V8.2 economy state and V8.3 time signals to recommend economy support actions.

## Inputs

- Business state.
- Market state.
- Exchange state.
- Bank state.
- Resource state.
- Transaction signals.
- Governance signals.
- Event stream.

## Recommendations

The advisor may suggest:

- Business expansion.
- Resource reallocation.
- Market stabilization.
- Bank reserve warning.
- Supply chain repair.
- Draft QA WorkOrder.

## Boundary

The advisor must not execute real trades, token transfers, lending, interest, custody, securities, payment processing or regulated financial services.
