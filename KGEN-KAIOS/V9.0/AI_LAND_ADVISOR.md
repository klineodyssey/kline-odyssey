# AI Land Advisor

**Document ID:** KAIOS-V9.0-AI-LAND-ADVISOR  
**Status:** Draft for Review / Prototype

## Purpose

The Land Advisor reads land development, residence capacity, resource state and civilization expansion pressure.

## Inputs

- Land state.
- Residence capacity.
- Population growth.
- Business demand.
- Resource reserve.
- Governance signal.

## Recommendations

The advisor may recommend:

- Land development.
- Residence expansion.
- Zoning review.
- Resource reserve review.
- Draft WorkOrder for map or schema inspection.

## Boundary

Land recommendations do not mint land, transfer land, claim real property rights or create regulated ownership instruments.
