# AI Policy Model

**Document ID:** KAIOS-V9.0-AI-POLICY-MODEL  
**Status:** Draft for Review / Prototype

## 1. Purpose

The AI Policy Model defines what V9.0 AI may and may not do. It protects Canon, protected paths, human authority and regulated boundaries.

## 2. Allowed Actions

AI may:

- Observe state.
- Analyze evidence.
- Reason about risks.
- Recommend actions.
- Generate Draft WorkOrders.
- Mark review requirements.
- Explain rejected alternatives.

## 3. Prohibited Actions

AI must not directly execute:

- Real financial transaction.
- Token transfer.
- Contract deployment.
- Protected path modification.
- Legal commitment.
- Brand partnership claim.
- Human account action.
- Main branch merge.
- Production deployment.

## 4. Source Rule

GitHub documents and machine-readable state are formal sources. Hidden chat memory, informal recollection or unstated assumptions may support context but cannot be the only evidence.

## 5. Review Rule

Any recommendation with R2 or higher risk must wait for Codex Review. R3 requires Codex and Human Review. R4 is blocked and may only create a warning and BLOCKED draft.
