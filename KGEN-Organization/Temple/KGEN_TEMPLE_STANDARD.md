**Maintainer:** KGEN Temple Office / Codex

## 1. Temple Definition

A temple is a living civilization organ, not a static building. Each temple has identity, image, AI, DNA, level, skill, service scope, and economic relationship.

## 2. One Image One Temple

一圖一神殿. A temple must not share its primary image with another temple. Visual reuse can happen only for archive or development notes, never for official public temple identity.

## 3. House

A house is the first building organ on land. It can evolve into shop, exchange counter, bank branch, warehouse, temple service node, or civic facility.

## 4. NPC

NPCs are living participants in temple and city systems. They can serve, trade, train, guard, teach, and evolve under governance rules.

## 5. Shop

A shop sells goods, App organisms, AI services, DNA, equipment, materials, land rights, temple services, and civilization technology.

## 6. Bank

A bank manages treasury concepts, reward distribution concepts, savings interfaces, and civilization finance reports. It must not contradict KGEN Token facts.

## 7. Exchange

An exchange handles market listings for App, Land, Temple, AI, DNA, and civilization assets. 11520 花果山交易所 is the official exchange center concept.

## 8. Warehouse

A warehouse stores resources, materials, equipment, records, and marketplace inventory. It must declare ownership and access rules.

## 9. Portal

A Portal connects temple, world, civilization, and universe paths. Portal entries must use official paths and cannot create hidden duplicate entry systems.

## 10. Organs

Temple organs include UI, runtime loader, wallet connector, bridge connector, AI chat, leaderboard, quest board, market panel, bank panel, image asset, and report log. Official organ filenames must be stable and must not include version suffixes such as v2, v3, final, hotfix, patch, or stable.

## 11. Evolution

Temple evolution may upgrade services, AI, DNA, visual state, civic role, market role, and Portal links. Evolution requires report, index update, and Codex Review.

## 12. Physical Link

A temple may map to real stores, brands, service sites, membership systems, or faith locations. Physical mapping is an extension and must respect local law and user safety.

## 13. Protected Temple 12345

`K線西遊記/temples/12345` is protected. It cannot be modified by Organization work unless a human gives explicit scoped approval.

## 14. Risk Rules

Temple changes must avoid duplicate initialization, duplicate event binding, CSS overrides, animation flicker, broken wallet flow, and image identity confusion.
