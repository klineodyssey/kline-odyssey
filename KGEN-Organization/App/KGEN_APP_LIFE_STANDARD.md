# KGEN App Life Standard

**Document ID:** KGEN-APP-LIFE-STANDARD-V2.0  
**Status:** Draft for Review  
**Level:** L3 Design Bible / L4 Runtime Reference  
**Maintainer:** KGEN App Office / Codex  
**GitHub Path:** `KGEN-Organization/App/KGEN_APP_LIFE_STANDARD.md`

## 1. Core Rule

App is not a tool. App is life. Every official App may have DNA, state, lifecycle, skill, owner, runtime connection, market status, and evolution path.

## 2. DNA

App DNA records identity, traits, compatibility, upgrade history, risk class, and inheritance rules. DNA must be machine-readable when exposed through SDK or marketplace systems.

## 3. Pairing And Reproduction

App organisms may pair or reproduce when governance, compatibility, and resource requirements are satisfied. Reproduction creates a new App life record and must preserve ancestor lineage.

## 4. Assembly

Assembly combines approved modules into one App organism. Assembly must declare module source, version-free official organ name, dependency, and rollback method.

## 5. Fusion

Fusion combines App traits into a stronger or specialized App. Fusion must define inherited traits, destroyed traits, cost, probability, and governance risk.

## 6. Disassembly

Disassembly breaks an App into reusable modules, materials, DNA fragments, or archive records. It cannot erase official history.

## 7. Evolution

Evolution changes App level, skill, AI connection, market value, or runtime capability. Evolution must be traceable through reports and metadata.

## 8. Trade

Apps may be sold, rented, licensed, or exchanged in 11520 花果山交易所 or approved civilization marketplaces. Trade must state ownership, transfer rights, use rights, and risk.

## 9. Death

App death means the App can no longer operate in active runtime. Death must preserve audit history and may produce materials, records, or inheritance tokens.

## 10. Rebirth

Rebirth restores or reconstitutes an App through approved rules. Rebirth cannot hide previous risk or ownership history.

## 11. Inheritance

Inheritance passes selected traits, DNA, memory, or skills to descendants. Inheritance must not clone prohibited assets or protected runtime secrets.

## 12. AI Organ

An App may connect to AI as an organ. AI must obey Canon, WorkOrder, and protected paths.

## 13. Marketplace

11520 花果山交易所 is the official marketplace concept for App organisms. Marketplace listing requires identity, DNA summary, risk, price, and ownership rule.

## 14. Risk Rules

App systems must prevent infinite reproduction, hidden privilege, unauthorized data access, and unreviewed runtime execution.
