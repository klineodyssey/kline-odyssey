## 1. Canon Purpose

This document is the Civilization Core Canon for KGEN Organization V2.0. It converts the creative world rules into operational rules for Codex, Cursor, future agents, runtime documents, SDK schemas, frontend portals, and publishing records.

## 2. Prime Laws

- 一圖一神殿。
- 一神殿一生命。
- 一土地一民宅。
- 一民宅可演化。
- 土地可探索、可建設、可交易、可戰爭。
- App 即生命。
- App 可組裝、可合成、可分解、可演化。
- NPC 可演化。
- 神殿可升級。
- 文明可升級。
- DNA and GA are the evolution cores of life, strategy, and application behavior.
- AI is a life organ and must obey Canon.
- KGEN is the on-chain economic medium of the civilization loop.
- 11520 花果山交易所 is the exchange center for App, Land, Temple, AI, DNA, and civilization assets.
- Universe Portal is the public entrance to the civilization universe.
- PrimeForge is the Mother Engine and source of official boot logic.

## 3. Life Chain

Universe -> Civilization -> World -> Temple -> Land -> Building -> NPC -> App -> AI -> DNA -> Runtime -> Code.

Every level may evolve, but no level may erase its ancestor rule. New documents may extend this chain only by adding compatible organs, stages, or interfaces.

## 4. Civilization Governance

Civilization governance is cumulative. It must keep public records, WorkOrders, review reports, protected paths, and Canon references. Cursor may construct and verify, but Codex must review and publish.

## 5. Economy Loop

Exploration -> Resource -> Land -> House -> Shop -> App -> AI -> DNA -> Trade -> KGEN -> Temple -> Civilization Technology -> Civilization War -> New Land -> Exploration.

The loop cannot be broken into isolated modules. A feature that creates assets must also identify its sink, market, governance rule, and risk boundary.

## 6. Game Loop

Explore, gather, build, produce, trade, accept quests, train, fight, upgrade, evolve DNA, evolve GA, build temples, occupy land, govern civilization, enter Portal, and explore new universe boundaries. Each step must create a clear next action for players.

## 7. AI Loop

AI reads Boot, Runtime CURRENT, Universe Map, AGENTS, Canon, WorkOrder, and protected paths. AI proposes, executes scoped work, reports, and waits for review. AI may improve implementation details, but cannot rewrite Canon or change project direction.

## 8. Blockchain Loop

KGEN token, wallet, bridge, contracts, exchange data, liquidity, burn, bank, reward, and AutoLP facts must remain consistent with KGEN V7.5.2 and official listing documents. Wallet-to-wallet transfers have no tax. AMM buy/sell tax is 0.30% only.

## 9. Extension Rule

Any new Civilization, Economy, Temple, Land, App, Runtime, SDK, or AI rule must link this document and state whether the rule is Canon, Design, Runtime, Implementation, Experimental, or Archive.
